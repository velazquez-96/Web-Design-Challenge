Max Temperature

As seen in the figure, the latitude of a city is approaching the equator in the southern hemisphere, the maximum temperature increase, and while latitude moves away from zero in the northern hemisphere the maximum temperature decreases. So, the results indicate higher temperatures close to the equator. However, it should be noted that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
